Credit Purchase Refunds

  • All credit purchases on WorkIndex are final and non-refundable once the transaction is completed successfully.
  • Credits are a digital consumable — once purchased, they are credited to your account and available for immediate use.
  • We do not offer refunds for unused credits simply because you changed your mind or decided not to use the platform.
  • Unused credits never expire — they remain in your account indefinitely and can be used whenever you're ready.
  • Credits cannot be transferred between accounts or converted to cash.
Important: Purchasing credits does not guarantee you will win clients or secure projects. WorkIndex is a lead generation marketplace — the outcome depends on your profile, pitch quality, and client preference. Unused opportunity is not grounds for a refund.

⚙️

Technical Failures & Payment Issues

  • If your payment was deducted from your bank/UPI but credits were not added to your WorkIndex account, this is considered a technical failure.
  • Such cases are reviewed individually and typically resolved within 24–48 business hours of your report.
  • If a duplicate payment was charged (the same transaction billed twice), the excess amount will be refunded.
  • Payment gateway fees charged by Instamojo are non-recoverable and may be deducted from any approved refund amount.
How to Report a Payment Issue: Log in → Go to Settings → Raise a Support Ticket → Select "Payment failed but amount deducted". Include your transaction ID, bank reference number, and the date/time of the transaction.
1

Report within 7 days

Technical payment failures must be reported within 7 days of the transaction date. Claims beyond this window may not be accepted.

2

Provide proof

Share your bank statement screenshot or UPI transaction reference number showing the deduction.

3

Review (24–48 hrs)

Our team verifies with the payment gateway. If confirmed, credits are added or refund initiated within 2 business days.

4

Refund processed

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method. Bank processing may take 3–7 business days additionally.

🚫

Cancellation Policy

  • Customers may cancel a posted service request at any time from their dashboard before a professional has been selected and work has started. Cancellation is free.
  • Professionals cannot "cancel" an approach once sent — the credits consumed for that approach are non-recoverable.
  • Account cancellation: You may request deletion of your WorkIndex account at any time by raising a support ticket. Any remaining unused credits in your account will be forfeited and not refunded upon account deletion.
  • WorkIndex reserves the right to cancel and refund a credit purchase if it determines the purchase was made fraudulently.
  • Subscriptions or recurring charges do not currently exist on WorkIndex — all purchases are one-time.
🤝

Service Quality Disputes

  • WorkIndex is a marketplace and does not guarantee the quality, accuracy, or outcomes of any professional service.
  • Disputes about the quality of work, deliverables, or timelines are between the client and the professional — WorkIndex is not a party to these agreements.
  • WorkIndex does not provide refunds for professional services paid to experts — those payments are made outside the platform.
  • If you have been defrauded or harassed by an expert, report it through the platform — WorkIndex will take account action including restriction or banning.
Best Practice: Always agree on deliverables, timelines, and payment terms with the professional before work begins. Payments made directly to professionals are outside WorkIndex's control.
