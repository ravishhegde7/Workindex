Frequently Asked Questions — ITR Filing Mangalore

I'm an NRI from Mangalore — do I need to file ITR in India? ▾
Yes, if your India-sourced income (rental income, NRO interest, capital gains from Indian assets, etc.) exceeds the basic exemption limit. NRI returns are more complex due to DTAA provisions and FEMA regulations. Mangalore CAs on WorkIndex have specific expertise in NRI tax matters.
Can I file ITR for my Mangalore property even if I live abroad? ▾
Yes. WorkIndex's online model means you can connect with a Mangalore CA from anywhere in the world. Share your documents digitally — rental agreements, property details, bank statements — and the CA will handle your ITR filing remotely.
What documents do I need for NRI ITR filing? ▾
PAN, Aadhaar (if applicable), NRI bank statements (NRO and NRE), foreign income details, property ownership documents, TDS certificates, and DTAA country of residence certificate. Your CA will provide a complete checklist tailored to your situation.
Does WorkIndex cover Udupi, Puttur, and other Dakshina Kannada areas? ▾
Yes. Since the entire process is online, CAs in Mangalore can serve clients from Udupi, Puttur, Bantwal, Sullia, Kasargod, and all of coastal Karnataka. Just post your requirement and mention your location.
What is the deadline for NRI ITR filing? ▾
Same as residents: 31 July for non-audit cases, 31 October for audit cases. Belated returns by 31 December. For NRIs with complex DTAA situations or foreign account disclosures, filing early is strongly recommended.
