Can a Mysore CA file my return if I'm now working in Bangalore? ▾
Yes. ITR filing is done online through the income tax portal, so there's no restriction on where your CA is located. A trusted Mysore CA you've worked with before can continue filing your returns even if you've relocated to Bangalore or any other city.
I'm a government employee in Mysore — do I still need to file ITR? ▾
If your income exceeds the basic exemption limit, yes. Even if TDS is deducted from your salary, filing ITR is mandatory above the threshold. It's also useful for refund claims, home loan applications, and visa purposes. Government employees typically qualify for simple salaried returns.
What is the ITR filing deadline? ▾
31 July for individuals (non-audit cases). 31 October for businesses requiring statutory audit. Belated returns can be filed until 31 December with a late fee under Section 234F (₹1,000 for income below ₹5L, ₹5,000 for above).
