FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — ITR Filing Bangalore

How much does ITR filing cost in Bangalore? ▾
ITR filing in Bangalore typically costs ₹999 to ₹15,000 depending on your income type. Salaried returns with Form 16 are the most affordable. Business and capital gains returns cost more. On WorkIndex you can compare quotes from multiple CAs before deciding.
Can I get my ITR filed online with a Bangalore CA? ▾
Yes. All CAs on WorkIndex offer fully online ITR filing. You share your documents digitally, the CA files on your behalf through the income tax portal, and you receive the acknowledgement (ITR-V) via email. No need to visit any office.
What documents are needed for ITR filing in Bangalore? ▾
For salaried: Form 16, PAN, Aadhaar, bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D, NPS), AIS from IT portal. For freelancers: add invoices, expense records. For business: P&L, balance sheet, GST returns. Your CA will provide a detailed checklist.
What is the ITR filing deadline? ▾
For individuals (non-audit cases): 31 July of the assessment year. For businesses requiring audit: 31 October. Belated returns can be filed up to 31 December with a late fee under Section 234F.
Old tax regime vs new tax regime — which should I choose? ▾
It depends on your deductions and income bracket. Most Bangalore IT employees with home loans, insurance, and 80C investments benefit from the old regime. Verified CAs on WorkIndex will calculate your tax under both regimes and recommend the one that saves you more.
Do I need to file ITR if my income is below ₹5 lakhs? ▾
Even if your tax liability is nil due to rebate under Section 87A, filing ITR is recommended. It helps with visa applications, loan approvals, carry-forward of losses, and serves as income proof. Many Bangalore professionals file even at low income for these benefits.
