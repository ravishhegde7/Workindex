FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — ITR Filing Karnataka

Can I hire a CA from a different Karnataka city for ITR filing? ▾
Yes. ITR filing is done entirely online through the income tax portal, so your CA's city doesn't matter. A CA in Bangalore can file your return even if you're in Mysore, Mangalore, or Hubli. WorkIndex lets you work with any verified CA across Karnataka.
Is ITR filing in Tier 2 Karnataka cities cheaper than Bangalore? ▾
Generally yes. CAs in cities like Mysore, Hubli, and Mangalore often charge lower fees than Bangalore CAs for similar services. Since the filing process is online, you can compare quotes from CAs across Karnataka and choose the most cost-effective option.
What documents do I need for ITR filing? ▾
For salaried: Form 16, PAN, Aadhaar, bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D), AIS from the IT portal. For business: P&L statement, balance sheet, GST returns. For NRI: foreign income statements, DTAA details. Your CA will provide a full checklist.
What is the ITR filing deadline for Karnataka residents? ▾
The deadline is the same nationwide: 31 July for individuals (non-audit), 31 October for audit cases. Belated returns can be filed until 31 December with a penalty under Section 234F. Post early to avoid last-minute delays.
